Nelia1

Application to simply track personal and incubating projects. Good place to stuff in all the great ideas you want to catch up later while keeping focus on your active projects.

This application features:
  • Sortable project list
  • Log (diary)
  • Self organising roadmap with minor and major milestones
  • Feature and issue issues tracking
Download Nelia1 in the Ubuntu Software Center
